Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

Description

These brownies are rich, fudgy, and dangerously delicious. They're quick to whip up and perfect for chocolate lovers craving a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Stir in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes. Do not overbake!
  6. Let cool, slice, and devour.
