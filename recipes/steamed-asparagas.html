Steamed Asparagus

Description

Steamed asparagus is a quick and healthy side dish that pairs beautifully with almost any meal. It’s crisp-tender, bright green, and delicious with just a touch of salt or a squeeze of lemon.

This simple method ensures your asparagus is never soggy or bland. A clean, green classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh asparagus, ends trimmed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: lemon wedges or a drizzle of olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and trim the tough ends of the asparagus stalks.
  2. Place a steamer basket in a pot with about 1 inch of water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the asparagus to the steamer basket and cover with a lid.
  5. Steam for 4–6 minutes, depending on thickness, until bright green and fork-tender.
  6. Carefully remove, season with salt, and serve hot with lemon or olive oil if desired.
